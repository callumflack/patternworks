Patternworks is the independent digital creative studio of Callum Flack and collaborators, located in Cairns, Australia. We plan, design & build for the web.

Our works are outcome focussed

Patternworks offers a wholistic approach to creating or updating digital businesses that is measurably successful and always looks good. We do this by understanding your business goals and your user's needs, planning for specific outcomes and executing detail upon detail. We believe digital products that are great user experiences are a better means of persuasion than any form of advertising.

We love making mobile-first, responsive websites for all device types. We specialise in ecommerce and blogging systems. We also partner with writers, photographers, videographers, content marketers and platform engineers.

About Callum

I have fifteen years experience in creative problem-solving: taking complex concepts and making them into simple, understandable and actionable stories. Whether through graphics, typography, copywriting, interaction or technology—the work is done in whatever mode will resonate.

I live with my wife and young family in the tropics of Cairns, far northern Australia, where I dehumidify my record collection weekly.

Experienced

Since starting in 2000, I've worked in design studios (Saatchi & Saatchi Design, London), inhouse startup teams (Box+Dice, Melbourne and WeLend, Hong Kong), and advertising (GPYR, Brisbane and Ward 6, Sydney). I've also co-run a record label (Roundtable Records). Lots of people have said nice things about working with me. You can read a few of them on LinkedIn.

If I was already a designer, why code?

By being able to build what I plan and design, I deeply understand how brand, usability and technical domains work together. This helps me to think in systems instead of web pages, turn blind spots into opportunity and stack up the small details that make big differences when creating great user experiences.
